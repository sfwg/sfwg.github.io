A Development Shop Everyone Can Work With

SF Working Group provides value to both early-stage startups and early-stage developers. Many early-stage startups do not have the revenue or resources to hire the typical consulting firms or established developers. On the other hand, early-stage developers have skills but lack professional experience to get hired yet.

Given the experience of our managing partner, Ken Decanio, as a skilled product manager, software architect and software master craftsman, he works with both sides to create the conditions where early-stage developers can produce professional applications with solid code on which early-stage startups and build their business at a price those startups can afford. This allows the developers to pay their bills while building the experience to work as a journeyman developer.
